Pranayama

Pranayama is a yogic practice involving controlled breathing techniques to enhance the flow of life force energy (prana) in the body. It promotes physical and mental well-being through rhythmic breath regulation.

Mindfulness Breathing

Mindfulness breathing involves focusing one's attention on the breath, fostering a heightened awareness of the present moment to promote relaxation and mental clarity.

Relaxation Techniques

In yoga, relaxation techniques involve conscious breath control and mental focus to calm the nervous system, promoting a state of deep relaxation and reducing stress.
